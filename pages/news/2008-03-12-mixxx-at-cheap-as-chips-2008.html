title: Mixxx at Cheap as Chips 2008 author: Albert date: 2008-03-12 00:19:00 {% extends "post.html" %} {% block post %} Mixxx was spotted last Sunday at the Cheap as Chips music festival in Christchurch, New Zealand. DJ Kid 90 dropped "a set of lush kiwi tunes to 10,000ish people", and was looking quite slick with his MacBook.







Kid 90 hosts an independent New Zealand electronica show on RDU , one of NZ's student radio stations. The show runs from 5 - 7pm Saturdays (NZ time), and he says he tends to play "Downbeat, Breaks and D'n'B with a little Psy thrown in for good measure".
