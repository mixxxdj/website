{% blocktrans with forums_link="/forums" support_page_link="/support" bug_tracker_link="https://bugs.launchpad.net/mixxx/" %}Support for users of Mixxx is available through our community forums and our IRC channel as described on our Support page. If you think you've found a bug, please file a bug report in our bug tracker.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans with mailing_list_link="http://lists.sourceforge.net/lists/listinfo/mixxx-devel" %}Developers working on Mixxx collaborate with each other via the mixxx-devel mailing list and our IRC channel (#mixxx on Freenode). To send an email to the list, you must subscribe first. The list is low traffic.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans with project_manager_link="hardware@mixxx.org" %}If you're a DJ hardware manufacturer and would like your product to work with Mixxx, please contact us at, hardware@mixxx.org. We accept donations and loans of DJ equipment which we use to improve both specific and general hardware support in Mixxx.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans with project_manager_link="press@mixxx.org" %}For other inquiries, please contact press@mixxx.org.{% endblocktrans %}
